I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the National University Of Singapore, hosted by Diptarka Chakraborty and Kuldeep S. Meel. I obtained my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Tata Institute Of Fundamental Research, Mumbai. Before that, I completed my B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Guwahati.

My research interest is broadly in algorithms and complexity with a focus on sublinear algorithms. Recently, my research has been centered on statistical estimation problems in small-sample regimes. Here is my resume.
